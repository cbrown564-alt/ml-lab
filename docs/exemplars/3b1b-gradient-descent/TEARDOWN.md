## Why it clears the bar

- **A composed 3D loss surface as the face of the lesson** (`00-viewport.png`): an undulating red cost surface with the descent path traced in dashed yellow, strong focal hierarchy, cinematic lighting. This is precisely the 3D treatment our roadmap defers to the deep-learning cluster — here is the concrete target to design toward.
- **Concrete data, not abstractions** (`01-scroll-20pct.png`): grids of handwritten MNIST digits with an explicit "Train on these / Test on these" panel. Generalization is shown with real instances before any formalism — intuition before math, the same order our `PRODUCT.md` mandates.
- **Register discipline:** dark cinematic frames for the surfaces, a calm light article body around them, one accent per figure. The atmosphere never becomes chartjunk.
